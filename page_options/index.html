What do you want to practice?

General Interview

This option provides a mock interview focusing on general questions that test your problem-solving skills, communication, and behavioral aspects.

The interview follows a flashcard style where you'll speak your answers. These spoken answers will be converted to text, allowing you to review and refine your responses later.

This feature helps you understand your thought process and improve your answers over time.

Technical Interview

This option includes a coding challenge with a live coding environment.

You will be presented with a coding problem, and before you start coding, you'll answer a series of questions designed to help you articulate your thought process.

This approach emphasizes not only solving the problem but also clearly explaining your reasoning and strategy, which are crucial skills in technical interviews.
